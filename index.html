Full Service Pools is a family owned and operated company, serving the southern midcoast area.
Our number one priority is that our customers are always satisfied with the quality of our work. We are
proud to offer the services of a larger company, while still being able to take care of each
coustomer's individual needs. We make sure to educate homeowners of what needs to be done, so
that you can do some services on your own if you wish.

Our services include...

  • spring openings
  • fall closings
  • maintenence of pool filters
  • product supply
  • water testing/treating
  • pool heater sales/service/installation
  • plumbing/heating/gas services

Some troubleshooting techniques you can do by yourself...

Re-circulation -- Running the pool water through the cycle, but skipping the filter. This is useful for dissolving chemicals.
Backwash -- Running the pool water through the cycle in opposite order. This is useful for cleaning the system. Running a backwash can
fix a heater problem in some cases.
